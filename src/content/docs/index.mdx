## Requirements

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Coordinator" icon="laptop">
    A project owner / coordinator who can pull all of the pieces together.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Drone Operators" icon="rocket">
    Users in the area of interest with drones, and willing to generate imagery. The drone must be a supported model.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Remote Mappers" icon="magnifier">
    A small team of digitisers, who generate a training dataset for AI models. If this includes people with local
    knowledge, even better!
  </Card>
  <Card title="Field Mappers" icon="farcaster">
    A group of local people who can verify features on the ground, adding tags to them with their local knowledge, via
    an easy to fill out mobile survey.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
